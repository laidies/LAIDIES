Use It Like An App

Put LAIDIES one tap away.

Add the site to your Home Screen so the weekly episode, dashboard, Fun Pack, quizzes, and playlists are not buried under twelve forgotten tabs.

iPhone / iPad
  1. Open the site in Safari.
  2. Tap Share.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Name it LAIDIES, then tap Add.
Android
  1. Open the site in Chrome.
  2. Tap More, the three dots in the top right.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen, Install app, or Create shortcut.
  4. Confirm Add or Install.
