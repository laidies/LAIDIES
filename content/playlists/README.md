# Playlists

Store playlist concepts, Spotify links, track lists, and notes for where each mix belongs.

Creative frame: this is the lAIdies version of the recurring 90s/2000s club scene. Think The Bronze on Buffy or P3 on Charmed: live-music energy, familiar people, low lighting, and the song that makes the episode stick. Each mix should explain the scene it belongs in, not just list songs.

Suggested fields:

- mix title
- Spotify link
- scene / when to press play
- three-song thesis
- episode tie-in, if any
- contributor credit
