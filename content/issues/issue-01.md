## Working Summary

AI is already becoming part of the invisible load at work, and lAIdies starts by making the first try small enough to actually do. The reader does not need a 40-hour course, a perfect company strategy, or a new identity as "technical." She needs to open the tab, try one low-risk task, compare tools, and keep her judgment.

## Core Lesson

Generative AI is not just "Google in a blazer." It can draft, summarize, compare, translate tone, and help turn messy work into a usable first pass. It still needs human judgment, context, and review.
