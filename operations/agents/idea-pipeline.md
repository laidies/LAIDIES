# lAIdies Idea Pipeline

Owner: Creative Strategy & Ideation Agent.

Purpose: capture new ideas for sections, games, quizzes, rituals, issue extensions, community prompts, content formats, partnerships, and experiments without letting novelty overload the live site.

## Rules

- Ideas are welcome; expansion is earned.
- Every idea needs a reader job, not just a clever reference.
- The smallest useful test comes before a full build.
- Product, Roadmap, Finance, Brand, Safety, and Reputation agents review ideas before they become public commitments.
- Parked ideas are not failures; they keep the launch focused.

## Review Questions

- Does this help a smart professional woman understand, try, remember, or share an AI concept?
- Is it unmistakably lAIdies, or could any AI brand do it?
- Does it strengthen an existing section before adding a new one?
- Can it be tested in one issue without creating a maintenance burden?
- What would prove that readers want more of it?
