# lAIdies Community Room Seed Posts

Use these as Ali starter comments before launch. Post one per room so the space does not feel empty, but leave room for readers to respond.

## Ask the Room

URL: `community/ask-the-room.html`

```text
Starter question: what is one work task you keep thinking AI "should" be able to help with, but you have not figured out how to ask yet?

Mine is usually the ugly first draft of something: turning scattered notes, a messy email chain, or a half-formed idea into a cleaner starting point. Bonus points if you share what tool you tried and where it went sideways.
```

## Send It Energy

URL: `community/send-it-energy.html`

```text
First tiny trick for the shelf: before asking AI to write something, I like giving it the job, the audience, and the standard.

Example:
"Act like a practical editor. Rewrite this for a senior stakeholder who wants the point quickly. Keep it direct, warm, and specific. Do not add facts I did not provide."

The magic is not the sentence. The magic is telling it what "good" means before it starts guessing.
```

## The Try-On Debrief

URL: `community/try-on-debrief.html`

```text
This week's try-on: take one email, paragraph, or rough note you already have and ask AI for three versions:

1. clearer
2. shorter
3. more executive

Then compare. Do not automatically use the output. Look at what changed. That is the practice.

Butterfly-clip rating: how many clips did it earn?
```

## Wins of the Week

URL: `community/wins.html`

```text
First win for the wall: starting counts.

If you opened the site, read one issue, tried one prompt, asked one question, or admitted you do not want to be left behind on AI, that is a real start. Tiny counts. Tiny repeated becomes the plot.
```

## Dear lAIdies

URL: `community/dear-laidies.html`

```text
Dear lAIdies starter prompt:

What is the workplace sentence you wish you had ready before the meeting got weird?

Examples:
- pushing back without sounding apologetic
- asking for clarity without sounding lost
- saying "that is not my understanding" without entering villain era
- following up when someone owes you an answer

Drop the situation, and the room can help draft the line.
```
