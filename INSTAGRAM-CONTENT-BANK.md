
## Posting Basics For Beginners

### How To Post A Reel

1. Open Instagram.
2. Tap the `+` button.
3. Choose `Reel`.
4. Record a vertical video or upload one from your phone.
5. Add on-screen text with the hook.
6. Add captions if Instagram offers auto-captions.
7. Pick a readable cover.
8. Paste the caption from this file.
9. Add 5-8 hashtags.
10. Tap `Share`.
11. Immediately share the Reel to Stories.
12. Check comments 10-20 minutes later and reply.

### How To Post A Carousel

1. Open Instagram.
2. Tap the `+` button.
3. Choose `Post`.
4. Select multiple images.
5. Upload slides in order.
6. Paste the caption from this file.
7. Add 5-8 hashtags.
8. Tap `Share`.
9. Share the post to Stories and add a poll or question sticker.

### How To Post Stories

1. Open Instagram.
2. Tap your profile photo or `+`.
3. Choose `Story`.
4. Add text or upload a simple graphic.
5. Use a poll, question sticker, quiz, or link sticker.
6. Post 3-5 frames in a sequence.
7. Reply to anyone who answers.

## Design Rules

Keep all graphics simple:

- hot pink headline
- blush background
- mocha text
- one image or icon max
- no tiny paragraphs
- one idea per slide

Canva slide size:

- Reels/Stories: 1080 x 1920
- Carousels: 1080 x 1350
