The Mix CD Exchange

The lAIdies club wall needs your mix CD.

The Bronze had bands. P3 had a stage. We have Spotify links, liner notes, DJ JAIDY requests, and women with excellent taste.

Drop a Spotify playlist link, the scene it belongs in, and three songs that explain the assignment. Or put in a song request for DJ JAIDY with the inspo, style, and scene. The best submissions can be added to the lAIdies club wall with credit.

Copy this format

Mix name: Friday Debrief, No Notes

Spotify link: paste the public playlist link

Scene: the meeting is over but your face has not reset

Three-song thesis: Lisa Loeb, The Cranberries, Michelle Branch

Credit: first name, full name, LinkedIn, or anonymous

DJ JAIDY request

Song inspo: an ode to Deb, who is terrible at her job but takes no shit from anyone

Style: 90s teen-drama ballad, Y2K pop anthem, dance mix CD, coffeehouse acoustic, or girl-power club scene

Scene: when this should play in the episode

Credit: first name, full name, LinkedIn, or anonymous
