lAIdies Card

Let the room know you.

A clubhouse intro with trading-card energy and actual utility.

Your lAIdies Card is a fun, optional way to show where you work, what you are trying with AI, what you want to learn, and how you might connect. You choose what is public, whether you want to share a LinkedIn or website link, what the card looks like, and whether you want to be considered for a future feature.

How It Works

Find your people. Trade the receipts.

This is not a vanity directory. It should help you see who is here, where each lAIdy is in her AI journey, what she is testing, what she needs, and who has the shortcut before everyone quietly Googles the same thing alone. The wrapper can still be fun: 90s trading-card energy, school-photo lasers, and a preview you approve before anything goes live.

01 Tell us the useful stuff

Role, journey stage, tools, support needs, help offered, location or time zone, and LinkedIn if you want to share it.

02 Pick your visibility

Public directory card, first name only, private for Ali/Savina, open to connect, or feature consideration.

03 Try the card looks

Upload a photo or choose a no-face stand-in, then preview the card modes. LAIDY or Mme CLAI-O can choose if you want a surprise.

04 Approve the one you want

The selected mode is saved with the profile. Before anything is published, the finished card comes back for final approval.

05 Then it goes where you allowed

Directory only, first-name version, private team view, or feature consideration. No mystery publishing. No midnight scramble.
