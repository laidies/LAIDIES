## Tools And Apps You Need

Start with the free setup. Do not buy a tool until you have posted at least two weeks of content.

1. Instagram professional account
   - Needed for scheduling and basic analytics.
   - Use either Creator or Business account type.

2. Meta Business Suite
   - Free.
   - Use it to schedule Instagram posts, Reels, Stories, and carousels from one planner.
   - Best first choice because it is native to Meta.

3. Canva
   - Use it to make carousel slides, Reel covers, and Story graphics.
   - Free is enough to start.
   - Canva Pro is useful only if you want Content Planner, brand kits, easier resizing, and more templates.

4. Phone camera
   - Record Reels vertically.
   - Use natural light.
   - Keep videos short: 15-35 seconds.

5. Optional video editor
   - Use Instagram's built-in editor, Meta Edits, CapCut, or Canva.
   - Keep this simple at first: trim, captions, readable cover.

6. Optional scheduler upgrade
   - Buffer, Later, Planable, or similar tools are only worth it if Meta Business Suite feels annoying or you want one calendar for Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and more.
   - Some tools can auto-publish; some features like trending audio, music, interactive stickers, and certain Story elements may still require manual publishing or notification publishing.

## Automation Recommendation

Use this stack first:

1. Canva for graphics.
2. Meta Business Suite for scheduling.
3. Instagram app for any Reel that needs trending audio, stickers, or final polish.

This is the most automated low-cost setup without overcomplicating the project.

## Beginner Weekly Workflow

Do this once per issue.

### Step 1: Open The Kit

Open the issue kit:

- Issue 01: `social/episodes/issue-01-instagram-kit.md`
- Issue 02: `social/episodes/issue-02-instagram-kit.md`

Do not start from scratch.

### Step 2: Make The Graphics

In Canva, make:

- 1 carousel with 7 slides
- 5 Story slides
- 3 Reel covers

Use one consistent look:

- big hot pink headline
- blush or light background
- mocha/dark body text
- lAIdies logo or wordmark
- one visual max per slide

### Step 3: Record The Reels

Batch record all 3 Reels for the issue in one sitting.

Use this setup:

- phone vertical
- natural light
- quiet background
- script pasted near your camera
- first sentence is the hook
- keep each Reel under 35 seconds

### Step 4: Schedule Everything

In Meta Business Suite:

1. Open Planner.
2. Create the first Reel.
3. Upload the video.
4. Paste the caption.
5. Add hashtags.
6. Choose Instagram.
7. Schedule it.
8. Repeat for the carousel and remaining Reels.
9. Schedule Stories if the tool allows the exact stickers/link behavior you need.

If the Story or Reel needs music, trending audio, interactive stickers, or precise Instagram-native styling, save it as a draft or use notification publishing instead of full auto-publish.

### Step 5: After Each Post

Within 10-20 minutes:

1. Reply to comments.
2. Reply to DMs.
3. Share the post to Stories.
4. Save the best reply or question for future content.

### Step 6: Friday Metrics Check

Track only these:

- Reels: views, shares, comments, follows.
- Carousels: saves, shares, profile visits.
- Stories: replies, poll votes, link clicks.

Do not overthink the first month. Repeat what gets saves, shares, and replies.

## Fastest Possible Version

If you are short on time, do this for each issue:

1. Post Reel 1.
2. Post the carousel.
3. Post the Story sequence.
4. Skip Reel 2 and Reel 3 until later.

That is enough to launch the issue socially.

## How New Issue Kits Are Generated

The episode JSON files are the source of truth. When a future issue is ready, refresh the generated social kits by running:

```powershell
node scripts/build-episode-assets.js
```

If the normal `node` command is blocked, use the bundled Codex runtime:

```powershell
& 'C:\Users\alieakin\.cache\codex-runtimes\codex-primary-runtime\dependencies\node\bin\node.exe' scripts\build-episode-assets.js
```

That regenerates:

- `content/episode-index.json`
- `social/episodes/issue-XX-instagram-kit.md`

## Tool Notes Checked June 2026

- Meta Business Suite is still the best free first scheduler for Instagram and Facebook planning.
- Canva Content Planner can schedule Instagram content for users with the right Canva plan/account connection.
- Buffer and Later support Instagram scheduling, but they are optional.
- Third-party schedulers may have Instagram API limitations. For example, trending audio, stickers, and some native Instagram features often work better when finished manually in Instagram.

Helpful references:

- Meta Business Suite: `https://business.facebook.com/`
- Canva Content Planner: `https://www.canva.com/pro/content-planner/`
- Buffer Instagram scheduling: `https://buffer.com/instagram/`
- Later Reel scheduling: `https://help.later.com/hc/en-us/articles/5023195813143-Schedule-Publish-Instagram-Reels`
