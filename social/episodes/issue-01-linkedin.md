## Post

AI fluency does not start with becoming technical.

It starts with one useful workplace try.

This week in lAIdies:
What AI is and how to have a first safe interaction with the major tools.

The work move:
"I do not need to understand everything to try one useful thing."

Try-On:
Open ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Give each the same low-risk work explanation prompt. Compare the outputs and pick the one that feels most useful.

Why it matters:
The one in which she realizes AI is already being added to the invisible load, says "ugh, as if," and opens the tab anyway.
