# Terry Tuesday

Weekly Tuesday teaser format for `@we.are.laidies`.

Use this to point people toward the Wednesday newsletter/issue drop with the Terry Crews / White Chicks "A Thousand Miles" reaction energy. The song is already in the lAIdies playlist, so this can become a recurring social bridge between the playlist, website, and weekly issue.

## Platform Guardrail

Use platform-native audio, remix, duet, stitch, or green-screen tools where available. Do not upload ripped copyrighted video or audio outside platform rules.

## Three-Loop Editing Note

The clip works better if it is not just one joke and out. Let the "A Thousand Miles" moment loop three times:

```text
statement [clip]
statement [clip]
statement [clip]
new issue drops Wednesday
```

Simple edit:

```text
1. Terry Tuesday setup card.
2. Statement 1 from tomorrow's issue.
3. Terry reaction.
4. Statement 2 from tomorrow's issue.
5. Terry reaction.
6. Statement 3 from tomorrow's issue.
7. Terry reaction.
8. New issue drops Wednesday / link in bio CTA.
```

Keep each statement short enough to read in one beat. The joke is that Terry keeps getting more locked in, and the final end card is the Wednesday drop.
