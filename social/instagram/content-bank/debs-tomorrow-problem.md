# Deb's Tomorrow Problem

Launch content for the Deb song.

## Core Line

## Voice Guardrail

Deb is beloved. The joke is not "older woman is bad at job." The joke is that every workplace has someone who has survived the system so completely that the normal rules no longer apply.

She may not be great at the official job description, but she is excellent at the real office job: forms, gossip, survival, printer emergencies, passive-aggressive calendar warfare, and knowing which rollout is about to become everyone's problem.
