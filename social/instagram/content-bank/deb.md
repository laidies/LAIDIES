# Deb Social Asset Bank

Reusable character asset for `@we.are.laidies`.

## Asset

Image: `social/instagram/assets/characters/deb-80s-portrait-stare-v1.png`

Source site asset: `assets/deb-80s-portrait-stare-v1.png`

Use when the post needs office-lore, reorg-survivor, early-retirement, "do not test me" energy.

## Character Note

Deb is the fictional office legend from the AI song notes. She is not the polished AI teacher. She is the person in payroll who has survived seven systems, three restructures, and every man named Brad who thinks his request is urgent.

## Best Uses

- Story reaction sticker for bad rollout energy.
- Reel character cold open before a practical AI lesson.
- Carousel cover for "AI things Deb would not tolerate."
- Website feature post pointing people to Member Cards, Dream Phone, or the eventual Deb song.
- Community prompt asking people what workplace task needs Deb energy.

## Design Notes

- Keep Deb large. The face is the joke and the hook.
- Do not crop out the glasses or folded-arms/desk energy.
- Pair with hot pink, teal, and mocha. Avoid beige corporate templates.
- Best frame styles: employee-of-the-month, office training VHS, trading card, payroll file, or hotline operator.
