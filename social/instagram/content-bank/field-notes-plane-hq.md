# Field Notes From lAIdies HQ: Plane Edition

Use these photos as founder BTS around building lAIdies in the margins.

## Best Image Picks

### Best Main Post Image

Use the laptop image with the lAIdies homepage open and the pink frosted snack on the tray table.

Why:

- shows the actual thing being built
- face is not central
- plane context is obvious
- snack adds lAIdies texture
- feels candid and credible

Suggested edit:

```text
Crop wide enough to keep the laptop, empty-seat setup, and pink snack.
Add a light Y2K grain or magazine field-note treatment.
Overlay text in one corner:
Field Notes from lAIdies HQ
```

### Best Story/BTS Image

Use the laptop + gummy candy + water image.

Why:

- very "work setup in the wild"
- shows the actual build process
- face-free
- good for a Story with a caption sticker

Suggested edit:

```text
Slightly brighten, increase contrast, crop so the laptop and candy are the story.
```

### Use Sparingly

The face/headphone photos can work as occasional founder texture, but they are not the strongest first share if Ali is not feeling face-forward.

Best use:

```text
photo strip, tiny corner crop, or BTS carousel slide 3.
```

The donut photo is funny and human. Use only if the caption makes the point:

```text
Plane snack. Noise cancelling. Building the thing anyway.
```
