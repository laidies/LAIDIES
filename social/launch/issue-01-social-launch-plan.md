# Issue 1 Social Launch Plan

Use this before posting the remaining Issue 1 launch content.

## Core Message

I do not want to wait for our lives to be over before women learn AI.

Translation for lAIdies:

- AI is being added to the invisible load.
- Women should not have to wait for a perfect training, a free weekend, or permission from the fleece-vest section of the office.
- Start with one low-risk prompt.
- Open the tab.
- Build fluency through small, useful tries.

```text
I do not want to wait for our lives to be over before women learn AI.

You do not need to become the IT department in better shoes.
You do not need a spare weekend.
You do not need to understand everything before trying one useful thing.

Issue 1 is about opening the tab, trying ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini on one low-risk work task, and noticing what actually helps.
```
