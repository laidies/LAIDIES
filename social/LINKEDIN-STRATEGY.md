# lAIdies LinkedIn Strategy

Use this for LinkedIn decisions and weekly posting.

## Recommendation

Use Alison's personal LinkedIn profile as the primary distribution channel.

Create a separate lAIdies LinkedIn Page as the official brand home, but do not depend on it for organic reach at the beginning.

## Why

The personal profile is better for:

- reach
- trust
- conversation
- comments
- personal founder voice
- getting busy professional women to actually stop and read

The lAIdies Page is better for:

- legitimacy
- search
- a clean brand destination
- future hiring, partnerships, press, and sponsorships
- reposting the best founder posts
- running paid promotion later if needed

## Operating Rule

Do not make LinkedIn another full content workload.

Weekly rhythm:

- 1 strong personal-profile post from Alison.
- 1 short repost or companion post from the lAIdies Page.
- Optional: 1 comment from the Page on Alison's post.
- Optional: ask collaborators to comment or reshare with their own take.

## What To Post From Alison's Profile

Use the personal profile for the posts that need a human voice:

- "I tried this at work..."
- "Here is what made AI finally click for me..."
- "This is what I wish busy corporate women were being told about AI..."
- "This week's lAIdies issue is about..."
- a useful prompt, workplace script, or lesson from the issue

Example:

```text
Prompting is not coding. It is delegation.

The first time I asked AI for help with a work update, I got back something so generic it could have been a LinkedIn motivational poster on a throw pillow.

The fix was not learning secret prompt engineering.
It was briefing the tool like a smart new hire:

- who this is for
- what they care about
- what tone I need
- what success looks like
- what not to include

This week's lAIdies issue is about being specific enough to get something useful back.

Read it here: [issue link]
```

## What To Post From The lAIdies Page

Use the Page for official brand posts:

- latest issue link
- website feature launch
- newsletter subscribe reminder
- community feature
- quiz/card pack/game announcement
- reposts of Alison's strongest posts

Example:

```text
Issue 02 is live: Tell Me What You Want.

This week: prompting as delegation, not coding.

Read the issue, take the quiz, and pull the Specificity Card.

[issue link]
```

## Page Setup

Create the lAIdies Page with:

- Name: lAIdies
- Tagline: AI fluency for women with full calendars, high standards, and no patience for beige tech explanations.
- Website: lAIdies homepage
- Industry: online media, education, professional training, or technology/information depending on available LinkedIn options.
- Logo: lAIdies logo.
- Banner: hot pink/mocha lAIdies website-style header.

## Do Not

- Do not post only from the Page and expect reach.
- Do not copy the full newsletter into LinkedIn.
- Do not make a separate LinkedIn Page if it means abandoning Alison's personal profile.
- Do not write Page posts in generic brand voice. Keep them useful, specific, and lAIdies.
