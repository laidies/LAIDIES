# lAIdies Reference Closet

Use this as the working bank for future analogies, public prompts, and reader requests. The rule: references should be widely known enough that the audience gets the joke quickly. David Rose is the standing exception because specificity is the point.

Taste filter: the canon should not rewrite Ali as the coolest person in the room. Favor references that feel remembered, accessible, mall/suburban/mainstream, workplace-adjacent, or lovingly specific over references that only read as retroactive cool-girl credibility.

Geography filter: Ali's memory bank is Canadian, so some U.S.-centric references may not feel personally vivid. That is fine; lAIdies will have users outside Canada, so broader North American/global 90s and Y2K references can belong when they are widely recognizable and useful.

## Image Generation Prompt Ingredients

Use these as styling ingredients when creating lAIdies visuals, member-card edits, game assets, or newsletter images. Combine one or two at a time; do not make every image wear the entire mall.

- 90s school-photo laser background, hot pink and teal light streaks, soft studio flash.
- Roll-on glitter on cheekbones, collarbones, or hands; keep it subtle unless the concept is intentionally chaotic.
- Bright Y2K blazer or power jacket, polished enough for work, loud enough to be fun.
- Newsboy hat, butterfly clips, glossy lip, or Claire's-style accessories as era cues.
- Early-2000s trucker-hat energy without readable logos.
- Layered preppy polos, lace-trim tank, hip belt, low-rise denim, flare jeans, platform sneakers, jelly sandals, baby tee, choker, belly-button piercing, or helix piercing as fashion-context references, used lightly.
- Dance Mix CD table setup: jewel case, handwritten track list, glitter pen, lip gloss, headphones.
- You've Got Mail desk: inbox, coffee, warm screen glow, message-writing tension.
- Practical Magic kitchen or apothecary mood: women helping women, candlelight, herbs, practical intuition.

Guardrails:

- Do not copy exact brand logos, copyrighted characters, or celebrity likenesses unless the asset is clearly commentary/reference and legally safe.
- Keep professional identity intact for member photos. Y2K styling should decorate the person, not transform her into someone else.
- Use the references to make the idea easier to understand, not to bury the lesson under nostalgia.
