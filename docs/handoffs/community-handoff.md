## Future Backend Needs

Forms and member profiles need a real backend or form provider before launch.

The AI Chat Room Digest also needs a backend/automation layer. The community pages currently use Hyvor Talk embeds. The digest should monitor Hyvor comments and organize:

- helpful answered questions
- useful tips
- unanswered questions needing a response
- repeated themes
- claims that need receipts or human review

See `docs/community/ai-chat-room-digest-plan.md` for the source-of-truth plan.

The member card source-of-truth flow is in `docs/community/member-card-flow.md`. Member cards should preserve the person and change only the card frame/background unless a member explicitly approves a future background-only AI edit.
