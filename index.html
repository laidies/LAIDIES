The Origin Story

Built by one woman, pushed forward by the right group chat.

Six months ago, I could not get an Amazon Women-in-Tax newsletter to send properly via email. I work in tax, not tech. I am not a developer. The ideas were there, but they kept getting stuck behind someone else's time, budget, or availability.

Then I started using AI. Since then, I have built a personal command centre, designed AI agents for research and operations, entered an Amazon Women-in-Tax hackathon with a team that built Echo, an AI product VPs now want to bring to production, made amazing lAIdies friends, and created the newsletter, website, images, and brand you are looking at now.

Use it like an app

Add lAIdies to your Home Screen.

Keep weekly packs, quizzes, playlists, and lAIdies Card progress one tap away. Until you sign in, scores and rewards are saved to the browser and device you use.

iPhone / iPad
  1. Open the site in Safari.
  2. Tap Share. On newer iOS versions, tap the Share button in the toolbar or open the More menu first.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen. If you see Open as Web App, leave it on.
  4. Name it lAIdies, then tap Add.
Android
  1. Open the site in Chrome.
  2. Tap More, the three dots in the top right.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen. Some Android versions say Install app or Create shortcut.
  4. Confirm Add or Install, then move the icon wherever you want it.
