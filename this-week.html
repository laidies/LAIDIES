Main Character Spritz

This week’s concept: Specificity. The more clearly you explain the task, audience, tone, constraints, and what “good” looks like, the less the AI has to guess. Vague prompt, vague output. David Rose would never.

  • 1 ½ oz blanco tequila
  • 1 oz Aperol
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • ¾ oz simple syrup
  • Top with Prosecco

Build over ice in a rocks glass, top with Prosecco, garnish with orange zest. Developed exclusively for lAIdies by Ryan C at CHAR No.5.
