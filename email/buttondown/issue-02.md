# Tell Me What You Want

The one in which she types something vague into ChatGPT, gets back garbage, and discovers the problem was never the technology. It was the ask.

This week:

How to write specific, context-rich prompts that produce usable work output.

Try this:

Give AI the same real task twice: once vaguely, then once with audience, context, tone, length, and what not to include. Compare the outputs.

Main Character Energy:

> I gave it context and it gave me back something I would actually use.
