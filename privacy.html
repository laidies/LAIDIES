Privacy

What the site saves.

lAIdies keeps the signup flow intentionally light. Clubhouse Pass asks for an email and optional profile answers such as work lane, AI experience, generation vibe, and what you want from the site. Those choices help shape future packs and community features.

Guest Mode

In guest mode, quiz scores, trading-card pulls, secret stickers, and Clubhouse Pass choices are saved in this browser using local storage. They do not follow you across devices unless Clubhouse Pass login is connected and you sign in.

Clubhouse Pass

When Clubhouse Pass login is configured, lAIdies uses Supabase to send the login email and store your Clubhouse Pass profile plus Clubhouse rewards like stickers, badges, trading cards, and quiz progress. Rewards are for fun and progress tracking, not verification or certification.

Newsletter

The newsletter is handled separately through Buttondown. During Clubhouse Pass signup, the newsletter checkbox is optional and off by default. You can also subscribe without creating a Clubhouse Pass from the standalone newsletter form.

Public Cards

Member cards are meant to help the room feel like a real community: easier introductions, shared interests, and more ways to find people building with AI too. Sharing is encouraged, but it should never feel required. Public sharing uses a separate card-builder approval step. Keep confidential work information, customer data, and anything private out of submissions.

Deletion Or Questions

For deletion requests or privacy questions, contact lAIdies through Instagram @we.are.laidies until the dedicated support inbox is added.
