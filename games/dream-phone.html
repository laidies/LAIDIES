Dream Phone

Dial a number by pressing the phone buttons or using your keyboard. Hyphens are optional; the phone will add them. Each caller gives different advice — career, drama, confidence, AI, leadership, operations, voice, or data.

Rumor has it one unlisted number unlocks a secret merit badge. Open your Clubhouse Pass so the phone knows where to pin it.

Pick a caller card, then dial the number with the phone buttons or your keyboard. Type 5551995 or 555-1995; both work.
