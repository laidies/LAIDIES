Use the room like a professional.

lAIdies is a learning site and community experience for practical AI fluency. The articles, games, prompts, and Clubhouse tools are educational and editorial. They are not legal, tax, financial, medical, or employment advice.

Community Submissions

Only submit material you have the right to share. Do not include confidential work information, customer data, private documents, or anything that would make another person reasonably uncomfortable.

Clubhouse Pass Rewards

Quiz stickers, trading cards, and badges are progress collectibles. They are designed to make practice more fun, not to certify expertise or rank members.

Public Sharing

Member cards are encouraged because the point is connection: finding other women learning AI, spotting shared interests, and making the community feel less anonymous. Sharing is optional and may be reviewed before publication. lAIdies can edit, hide, or remove submissions that are off-topic, unsafe, misleading, or inconsistent with the community purpose.

AI output still needs human judgment. Check facts, protect sensitive information, and review anything important before using it at work.
